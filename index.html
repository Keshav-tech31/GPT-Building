COLUMN

INTRODUCTION

A column is a vertical structural element that supports compressive loads from beams and slabs and transfers them to the foundation. It plays a crucial role in the stability of a structure.

TYPE OF COLUMN

BASED ON SHAPE

  • Rectangular Column
  • Square Column
  • Circular Column
  • L-Shaped Column
  • T-Shaped Column
  • Hexagonal & Octagonal Column

BASED ON MATERIAL

  • Reinforced Concrete Column (RCC Column) – Made of concrete with steel reinforcement, commonly used in buildings.
  • Steel Column – Entirely made of steel, used in industrial structures and high-rise buildings.
  • Timber Column – Made from wood, used in traditional and temporary structures.
  • Brick Column – Constructed using bricks, used in old masonry buildings.
  • Stone Column – Made from stones, found in ancient and historic buildings.

BASED ON REINFORCEMENT TYPE

  • Tied Columns – Contain lateral ties to hold vertical reinforcement bars in place.
  • Spiral Columns – Have helical reinforcement for extra strength, used in earthquake-prone areas.

RECTANGULAR COLUMN

A rectangular column is a vertical structural element with a rectangular cross-section that primarily carries axial loads (compression) and sometimes bending moments. It is commonly used in buildings, bridges, and other structures.

BEAM

INTRODUCTION

A beam is a horizontal structural element that carries and distributes loads from slabs, roofs, or walls to columns and foundations. It primarily resists bending and shear forces and ensures the stability of a structure.

TYPE OF BEAM

DETERMINATE BEAM

  • CANTILEVER BEAM
  • SIMPLY SUPPORTED BEAM
  • OVERHANGING BEAM

INDETERMINATE BEAM

  • CONTINUOUS BEAM
  • FIXED BEAM
  • PROPPED BEAM

SIMPLY SUPPORTED BEAM

A Simply Supported Beam is a structural element that is supported at both ends but is free to rotate and does not have any moment resistance at the supports. One end typically has a pinned support, which prevents horizontal and vertical movement but allows rotation, while the other end has a roller support, which allows horizontal movement and rotation but prevents vertical movement.
